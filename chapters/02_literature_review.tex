\section{Chapter overview}

The application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in financial markets is rapidly increasing, transforming a domain that has traditionally relied on manual, time-consuming, and repetitive processes [7]. 

Financial market analysis literature can broadly be divided into two main categories: technical analysis and fundamental analysis. Technical analysis (TA) focuses on identifying market trends and price patterns to forecast future price movements. In contrast, fundamental analysis (FA) seeks to determine the intrinsic value of a company by examining its financial performance, business fundamentals, and the influence of external economic and industry-related factors. Technical and fundamental analysis are both equally important and complementary approaches in financial market analysis [8][10].
